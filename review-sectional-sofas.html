How to Choose the Right Sectional Sofa

Sectionals are a big investment and a big piece of furniture. Getting it wrong is expensive. Here's what to think about before you buy:

📐 Measure First
Always measure your room before ordering. Leave at least 18 inches between the sofa and other furniture. Measure doorways too — many sectionals require assembly inside the room.
🐾 Pets & Kids
If you have pets or kids, look for performance fabrics or removable washable covers. Chenille and velvet look beautiful but show wear faster than microfiber or linen blends.
☁️ Firm vs Soft
Boneless cloud sofas are ultra-comfortable but some people find them too soft for long-term back support. Traditional foam cushions offer more structure and hold their shape longer.
🔄 Chaise Direction
Most sectionals come in left-facing or right-facing chaise configurations. Stand in your room and decide which side feels natural before ordering — returns on large furniture are costly.
