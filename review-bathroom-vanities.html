Bathroom Vanity Buying Guide

Before buying a bathroom vanity there are a few critical measurements and decisions to get right:

📐 Size & Fit
Measure your bathroom carefully before ordering. Standard vanity depths are 18-21 inches. Make sure you have clearance for the door to open and access to plumbing.
🚰 Sink Included?
Many vanities don't include a sink or faucet. Check what's included before buying — a complete package saves significant cost and installation complexity.
💧 Moisture Resistance
Bathroom humidity is tough on furniture. Look for solid wood or MDF with moisture-resistant finishes. Avoid particleboard in high-humidity bathrooms.
🔧 Installation
Most vanities require some plumbing knowledge to install. If you're not comfortable with plumbing, budget for a plumber — it typically costs $150-300 for installation.
