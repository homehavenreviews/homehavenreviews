Smart bulbs are one of the easiest and most affordable ways to upgrade your home. But with Philips Hue, LIFX, Govee, and dozens of lesser-known brands all competing for your wallet, it's hard to know which ones are actually worth it. We broke down the top three options so you can pick the right one for your home and budget.

The starter kit includes a bridge and two bulbs, giving you instant access to the full Hue ecosystem — scenes, automations, voice control, and more. Our Verdict

It depends on how deep you want to go.

If you want the best smart home lighting experience and plan to expand, go with Philips Hue — it's worth the investment. If you just want a few color-changing bulbs without spending much, Govee is genuinely impressive for the price. And if you want premium quality without a hub or ecosystem lock-in, LIFX is the sweet spot.

Smart Bulb Buying Guide

Before picking a smart bulb, think about how you plan to use it and which smart home platform you already use:

🏠 Hub vs No Hub
Hub-based systems like Philips Hue are more reliable and responsive. WiFi bulbs like Govee and LIFX are easier to set up but can occasionally lag or drop connection.
🗣️ Voice Assistant
All three work with Alexa and Google. Only Philips Hue and LIFX work with Apple HomeKit. If you're in the Apple ecosystem, avoid Govee.
🌟 Brightness
Look for lumens, not just watts. For a standard room you want at least 800 lumens. LIFX leads here, followed closely by Philips Hue.
💰 Cost Per Bulb
Govee is cheapest per bulb (~$7). LIFX runs ~$22 per bulb. Philips Hue is ~$20–$25 per bulb after the initial hub investment.
