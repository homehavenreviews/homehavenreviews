What to Look for in a Platform Bed Frame

Platform bed frames vary a lot more than people realize. Here are the key things to consider before buying:

📏 Slat Spacing
Slats should be no more than 3 inches apart for proper mattress support. Wider gaps can void your mattress warranty and cause sagging over time.
📦 Under-Bed Storage
Measure the clearance before buying if storage matters to you. Metal frames typically offer more clearance than upholstered ones.
🔇 Noise & Squeaking
Metal frames can squeak over time. Upholstered wooden frames tend to be quieter. Look for reviews mentioning long-term noise performance.
⚖️ Weight Capacity
Most standard frames hold 500–750 lbs combined. If you need more, look for reinforced center support legs, especially on queen and king sizes.
