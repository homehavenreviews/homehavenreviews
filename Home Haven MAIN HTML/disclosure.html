Transparency

Affiliate Disclosure

We believe in being completely upfront about how this site makes money.

🤝

The short version

Some links on HomeHaven Reviews are affiliate links. If you buy something through one of those links, we earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site free and independent. It never affects what we recommend.

What is an affiliate link?

An affiliate link is a special tracked URL. When you click it and make a purchase, the retailer records that you came from our site and pays us a small percentage of the sale as a commission. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went directly to the retailer.

Which programs do we participate in?

  • Amazon Associates — the most common program we use. Commissions range from 1–10% depending on category.
  • Other retailer programs — we may also include affiliate links from Wayfair, Home Depot, Lowe's, and other home and hardware retailers.

Not every link on the site is an affiliate link. We link to the best source for each product regardless of whether we earn anything from the click.

Does this affect our recommendations?

No. Our reviews and recommendations are based entirely on research, product quality, value, and real customer feedback — not on which products earn us the highest commission.

We will never recommend a product we don't genuinely believe is worth buying. If a product has serious flaws, we say so in the review, even if that costs us a commission.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is made in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials (16 CFR Part 255). We are required by law to disclose any material connection between ourselves and the products we recommend.

Every review page and article on this site that contains affiliate links includes a disclosure notice at the top of the page.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how we choose products to review, please reach out via our contact page. We're happy to be transparent about anything.
