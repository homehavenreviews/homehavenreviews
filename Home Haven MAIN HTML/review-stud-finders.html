Stud Finder Buying Guide

Not all stud finders work the same way. Here's what to know before buying:

📡 Sensor Count
More sensors means more accuracy. Single-sensor models require careful sweeping technique. Multi-sensor models like the Franklin detect stud edges instantly.
⚡ AC Wire Detection
Always choose a stud finder that also detects live AC wiring. Drilling into a live wire is dangerous. Most mid-range and above models include this feature.
🧱 Wall Thickness
Standard drywall is 1/2" thick. If you have older plaster walls or tile over drywall, look for a model rated for walls 1.5" or thicker.
🔋 Calibration
Most stud finders need calibration before each use — hold flat against the wall with no stud beneath. The Franklin ProSensor skips this step entirely.
