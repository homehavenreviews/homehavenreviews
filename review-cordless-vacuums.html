Cordless Vacuum Buying Guide

Not all cordless vacuums are equal. Here's what actually matters when choosing one:

🔋 Battery Life
Look for at least 30 minutes of runtime on standard mode. Max suction mode drains batteries fast — check both ratings before buying.
💨 Suction Power
Measured in Pa (pascals) or watts. For carpet you need at least 20,000 Pa. For hard floors and light use, 15,000 Pa is sufficient.
🐾 Pet Hair
If you have pets look specifically for a self-cleaning or tangle-free brush roll. Hair tangles are the number one frustration with cheap cordless vacuums.
⚖️ Weight
Lighter is better for daily use. Most cordless vacuums weigh 3-6 lbs. If you clean stairs or do a lot of above-floor cleaning, under 4 lbs is ideal.
